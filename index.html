About Me



Lance is a Filipino-born and raised Geospatial Technician working for the Las Vegas Valley Water District in Las Vegas, Nevada. He focuses on supporting infrastructure data and mapping by capturing, verifying, and editing as-built facilities construction and maintenance projects. Before moving back to the civilian side of things, he served five years in the US Air Force as an Engineering Assistant with years of experience doing GIS, AutoCAD, and Surveying. Lance is currently completing his master's degree in GIS and Web Map Programming from the University of Wisconsin, on track to graduate summer of May 2022. Lance is a long time "Faithful" of the San Francisco 49ers and enjoys watching all sports in general. In his spare time, he enjoys his Thai Tea with boba, traveling, hiking, playing basketball, board and brain games.
